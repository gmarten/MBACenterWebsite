MBA Center Brussels

MBA Center Brussels is one of fifteen MBA Centers in the world. It was the third to open in Europe, after Paris and Frankfurt.Created in 1996 by Dr. Hubert Silly, a pioneer in educational communication, test prep, and admission consulting.

MBA Center was designed to help European students score high on tests and polish their university applications, in order to increase their chances of admission to their program of choice.

With 30,000 students trained over the course of 15 years, MBA Center has gained a strong reputation in the education industry.

MBA Center books are published or distributed by 50+ publishers, including Hobsons, Hachette, Studyrama. Our group has developed a strategic partnership with Educational Testing Services, and hosts and runs the TOEFL and the TOEIC.

MBA Center hosts MBA presentations with prestigious business schools, including Stanford, Wharton, NYU, Columbia, INSEAD, and HEC.

We are the official test prep sponsor of QS and ACCESSMBA in Brussels. And MBA Center has developed a strong partnership with Vesalius College, and works to prepare its senior students for the GMAT.
