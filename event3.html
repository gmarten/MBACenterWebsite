Student of the month!



Tickets

Saturday January 10, 2015

Event Info

Each month MBA Center selects a student who has been particularly successful for a 2-hour presentation. The seminar is moderated by Hubert Silly, our in house specialist. It starts with a 15-minute presentation of the student and is followed by a Q&A session where MIM, MBA and EMBA students interact with the student and Hubert Silly.

We will invite Michel Straga (Chicago Booth MBA’2015)

The objective of this seminar is to share the student experience and understand the do’s and don’ts. We will insist a lot on school selection, on the timing, on the sacrifice that students make to undergo an MBA, as well as the financial considerations when undertaking graduate business school programs.

We offer you to get an inside experience from a student who has successfully and recently completed a business school program admission process. We will also give you the opportunity to interact with Hubert Silly who will give you a broader view of the admission process in a graduate business school. Finally, for those who are interested we can schedule a face to face session during which we can help with school selection, study plan, and financial aid.
