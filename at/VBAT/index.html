MBA Center Brussels

MBA Center Brussels was open in 1998 and is one of the fifteen MBA Centers in the world.

MBA Center is perfectly integrated in the Belgian educational landscape as we plan to send in 2014/2015 about 40 students to the top Belgian business schools (Vlerick Business School, Solvay, Leuven, and Antwerp Management School)

MBA Center is also a key player in the test prep-industry as we teach the GMAT and the SAT in about 15 institutions in Belgium and send students to top educational institutions in the world (LBS, LSE, HEC, City University, Imperial College, etc)

As an actor who wants to promote access to tests, MBA Center is also working partnership with test providers to administer their tests (TOEFL, TOEIC mainly)

Finally, MBA Center is a sponsor of events such as QS, Access MBA and MBA World Tour, where business schools and potential business students come together to discuss the latest options and curricular developments. MBA Center also regularly hostsVlerick Business School, INSEAD, HEC, ESADE and IE.

Study with Test Experts

MBA Center only hires test experts who score high on the business test taughtin their department, and who participate in the company’s publishing program.

The books and material written by MBA Center’s very own prep masters are recognized for their quality and published by the largest educational publishers in Europe.

MBA Center Brussels runs test prep courses at top European establishments, including ESSEC (France), EBS (Germany) and Politecnico di Milano (Italy).

Unlimited Number of Teaching Hours

Our VBAT test-prep course lasts 12 hours, running2 week-ends before the test. We also run regular VBAT test simulations..Every student who take part of /to our class, is allowed to renew as many times as he/she wishes during 12 months.

Material developed by our own experts

MBA Center has a great expertise preparing for standardized tests. Last summer after getting very strong success rate at Vlerick, we have decided to develop specific curriculum for the VBAT including lessons practice questions and practice tests.

Comprehensive Service

The VBAT admission test is the major hurdle to overcome when applying to Vlerick Business School. MBA Center’s Premier Coaching Department both prepares applicants for the test, and helps them with their application: improving their CV, letters of reference, application essays and interview approach.
