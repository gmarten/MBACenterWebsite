MBA Center Brussels

The MBA Center Brussels is one of the oldest MBA Centers in the world; it was created in 1998 and has trained thousands of students to the TOEFL, IELTS, SAT, Solvay test and GMAT.

The MBA Center Brussels is a gateway to top European and US business schools, with hundreds of students already accepted at top universities and business schools. The MBA Center Brussels is known for its outstanding success rate in BAs, MBAs, EMBAs and MIMs with students accepted at Harvard, NYU, INSEAD, LBS, IE, IESE, HEC, ESCP Europe, Solvay Business School…

In order to get such academic great results, the MBA Center has developed a unique expertise to allow its students score high on standardized tests such as the GMAT, SAT, TOEFL, TOEIC, Solvay test and IELTS. The MBA Center offers courses adapted to each your needs: group classes, online classes and private tutoring.

Thanks to its reputation, MBA Center is also the official partner of Educational Testing Services (TOEFL, SAT, GRE…) and one of the sponsors of the QS World MBA Tour and the College Day.

Study with Test Experts

MBA Center Brussels hires instructors who have scored high on the IEGAT and who are part of its R&D Department. The instructors are experts and also participate in the books that we have published through top French publishers: Studyrama, Hachette Livres, and Ellipses.

The MBA Center courses are recommended by ESSEC (France), EBS (Germany) and Politecnico di Milano (Italy), all of whom use our test-prep services

Unlimited Number of Teaching Hours

Our intensive course lasts 24 hours of teaching and can be extended on demand.

For the students who want to know more about the MBA Center before registering with us we organize rigorous mock tests at our center throughout the year.

Material developed by our own experts

MBA Center is the premier test prep center in Europe and has developed a unique methodology preparing for standardized tests. Our approach relies on knowledge acquisition, skills development and intensive practice.

The MBA Center has decided to develop specific curriculum for the IEGAT test including lessons test-like practice questions and tests.

This approach has served us well and allows us to have 100% success rate at the IEGAT test.

Comprehensive Service

The IEGAT entrance test is not the only hurdle to overcome when applying to the Instituto de Empresa. Indeed in addition to the test the Instituto de Empresa requires an interview, essays and letters of reference.

MBA Center’s Premier Coaching Department both prepares applicants for the test, and helps them with their application improving their CV, letters of reference, application essays and interview approach.
