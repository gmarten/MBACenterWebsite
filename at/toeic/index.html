MBA Center Brussels

The MBA Center Brussels is one of the oldest MBA Centers in the world; it was created in 1998 and has trained thousands of students to the TOEFL, IELTS, TOEIC, SAT, Solvay test, VBAT and GMAT.

The MBA Center Brussels is a gateway to top European and US business schools, with hundreds of students already accepted at top universities and business schools. The MBA Center Brussels is known for its outstanding success rate in BAs, MBAs, EMBAs and MIMs with students accepted at Harvard, NYU, INSEAD, LBS, IE, IESE, HEC, ESCP Europe, Vlerick Business School, Solvay Business School…

In order to get such academic great results, the MBA Center has developed a unique expertise to allow its students score high on standardized tests such as the GMAT, SAT, TOEFL, TOEIC, Solvay test and IELTS. The MBA Center offers courses adapted to each your needs: group classes, online classes and private tutoring.

Thanks to its reputation, MBA Center is also the official partner of Educational Testing Services (TOEFL, SAT,GRE…) and one of the sponsors of the QS World MBA Tour and the College Day.

Study with test experts

MBA Center only hires teachers who are professionally certified (CELTA, TESOL, TEFL), and who participate in our publication program. The output from these development projects include books published by the largest educational publishers in Europe (Hobsons, Studyrama, Hachette Livres, Ellipses…)

Finally the MBA Center courses are recommended by ESSEC (France), EBS (Germany) and Politecnico di Milano (Italy), all of whom use our test-prep services.

Unlimited Number of Teaching Hours

Material developed by our own experts

MBA Center is the premier test prep center in Europe and has developed a unique methodology preparing for standardized tests. Our approach relies on knowledge acquisition, skills development and intensive practice.

Comprehensive Service

The IELTS is used to test the English level of non-native English speakers who are applying to universities world-wide, as are other entrance tests such as the GRE and GMAT; MBA Center also runs similar course for these tests.

MBA Center’s coaching experts guide students through the process of creating well prepared CVs, applicationessays and letters of reference, with the final stage being realistic university admission interviews.
