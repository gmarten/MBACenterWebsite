Event Info

MBA Center offers a 75-minute test comprising 18 verbal (Sentence Correction, Critical Reasoning and Reading Comprehension questions) and 18 math questions (Problem Solving and Data Sufficiency). This test is followed by a 75-minute group correction session with Hubert Silly who has designed the test.

The objective of the GMAT kit is threefold:

  1. Get familiar with GMAT structure
  2. Learn about the difficulty of the GMAT
  3. Assess your GMAT level

The GMAT toolkit will also give you an opportunity to meet our instructors, determine a study plan with them as well as choose the courses that we have that are most appropriate to your objectives. This is also an opportunity to come and meet us at our facilities.
