Eirini Digka

Biography

Eirini Digka is in charge of organizing events at the MBA Center Brussels. As such she has two main responsibilities. Organizing the 1-2-1 meetings with schools that the MBA Center organizes each year between January and September and launching MBA Center Brussels Master Class, an event that is jointly organized by the MBA Center and top business school such as INSEAD, LBS, IE, ESADE, Solvay and Vlerick to name a few.

Eirini comes from Thessaloniki where she has lived and studied. She majored in Economics and International Business and minored in Slavic studies. Fluent in English, Russian and Bulgarian,

Eirini is a person who has great social skills.

Before working for the MBA Center Eirini had two work positions the first was at the Consulate of Kosovo in Thessaloniki where she was in charge of communication and events of the Consulate and the second one was with the Egyptian representation in Thessaloniki where she was in charge of promoting trade between Egypt and Greece.

An accomplished woman, Eirini has competed in athletics for four years, she is an accomplished 400-meter runner. Eirini is also a passion by arts. Her hobby is acting and she has played in different plays. She is currently preparing a play in Brussels and will be on stage from March 19 to March 22.
